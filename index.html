About

Sherlock Holmes is a fictional detective created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. He is known for his exceptional intelligence and his ability to solve complex cases through observation and logical deduction. He is accompanied by his friend and chronicler, Dr. John Watson, with whom he shares a flat at 221B Baker Street, London.
